EGG TOAST RECIPE


Tired from fried egg with bread? What if it was to be...combined as one like literally? This once again, simple dish takes that definition and it tastes even much more delightful just from the ingredients from your home.

Creating the dish is not difficult at all. It follows the same process as when you fry an egg just that now it is combined with the slices as on. This will follow a process of making egg toast of only 2 slices, the type of flavoring to use is up to your preference.

INGREDIENTS

  • Bread
  • 2 Eggs
  • Flavoring or Spices
  • Cooking Oil
  • Butter (optional)

STEPS

  1. Switch stove on to medium heat and pour 2 tablespoons of cooking oil
  2. Until oil sizzles, mix egg with mixer or spoon with preferred flavoring in this instant Aromat (optional).
  3. After mixing, pour mixture on flat surface to lay each slice on both sides.
  4. Lay each slice coated with the egg letting each side fry for 40-50 seconds
  5. When slices are ready, you can put on toast for at least 7-8 minutes and add tomatoes if you want.
