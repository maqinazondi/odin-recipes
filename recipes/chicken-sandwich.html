Chicken Sandwich Recipe




Toasted Chicken Sandwich


The toasted chicken sandwich famous for its delicious simple taste, very easy to make at home and always serves a satisfying taste. Founded by Truett Cathy in 1964 this dish was known as Christian food but evolved as time went by. This page will simply assist in directing you on how to make this flawless dish so that you can have a dish worth history satisfying your hunger.


Ingredients


  • Chicken
  • Creamy Spread
  • Bread
  • Butter
  • Flavorings

For your ingredients, you can also add your own type of ingredients and they can be the leftovers you may have in the fridge does not have to be bought if you have it at home.

STEPS

  1. Have all your required ingredients close by.
  2. 1-2 Tablespoons of Creamy Spread in bowl(Mayonnaise, yoghurt up to you).
  3. Add shredded lettuce and mix well with creamy spread.
  4. Add your preferred seasonings and flavors to mix.
  5. Add your shredded chicken to mix(chicken can be any brand that is suitable for consumption)
  6. Toast your two slices and spread with butter (optional) and mix your chicken mayo mixture.
  7. When slices are ready, spread with chicken mayo (up to you how many slices you may want to spread)

Of course such sandwiches come in different forms it can be with buns instead and may even include tomatoes
