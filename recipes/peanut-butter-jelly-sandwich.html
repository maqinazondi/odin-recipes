Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich




This delightful yet simple dish has existed for as long as humankind can remember what makes this so special is how simple it can be made at home with your ordinary ingredients not needing much of a budget to really make such a dish.

This dish suits almost any age but i do recommand that u do not attempt to make it if you have an allergy to peanuts as it without a doubt has to have peanut butter as a part of it. The ingredients below are not specified to a certain brand of product, even if you have a different brand for each ingredient that is neccessary for dish it will be okay as long as it is suitable.


INGREDIENTS

  • Butter
  • Jelly (strawberry)
  • Bread (2 slices)
  • Peanut Butter

STEPS

  1. Take out your two slices of bread, spread them wth your preferred butter (optional).

  2. When slices properly spread with butter put both slices seperately in toaster for 3-4 minutes.

  3. When properly toasted, spread one side of slice with peanut butter and the other with jelly, i recommend strawberry flavor

If you preferred a different flavor for jelly it is acceptable after all, dishes are made to be enjoyed with satisfaction.
